Q&A

Below are some of the most frequently asked questions that we at FBLA recieve. We use our experience to bring knowledge to any student willing to show interest in our organization.
FBLA?
What is FBLA?

WHY FBLA?
Why would I want to do FBLA?

The number one reason is that FBLA is Fun! FBLA is a great way to make new friends and to hang out with friends you already have.

BUSINESS?
I'm not really interested in Business. Can I still Join?

Of course!! Business may not be for everyone, but FBLA still has something for you. At AC FBLA, we do activites for everyone, so everyone will have fun in this chapter!

HOW TO JOIN
How would I join FBLA?

Attend the meeting at the beginning of the school year and pay dues to {{ site.officers.advisor }} by the due date. Simple as that!

WHAT CAN I DO?
If I join FBLA, what would I have to do?

FBLA is whatever you want to make it. You could simply attend and have fun at the meetings, or you could volunteer, attend state and possibly qualify for nationals.
